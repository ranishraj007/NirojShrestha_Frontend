# Niroj Shrestha

> Community Leader • Educator • Change Maker

Empowering Communities Through Education and Sustainable Development.

Niroj Shrestha is a community educator and social worker dedicated to uplifting youth, women, and farmers through learning, innovation, and sustainable action.

## About Niroj

Born and raised in Pharping, Kathmandu Valley, Niroj Shrestha has devoted his life to creating pathways for education, empowerment, and sustainable development in rural Nepal.

As the founder of Shikharapur Community Campus, he has transformed how communities access learning—championing education for youth, economic empowerment for women, and sustainable agriculture for local farmers.

His work bridges local tradition with global innovation, having participated in youth exchange programs in Japan and leadership initiatives in the United States, bringing world-class insights back to grassroots action.

## Key Focus Areas

*   **Education:** Transforming Rural Education through building educational infrastructure in remote communities and exploring the power of grassroots initiatives.
*   **Development & Empowerment:** Uplifting youth, women, and farmers. Empowering women entrepreneurs to start businesses and support their families.
*   **Sustainability:** Promoting sustainable agriculture and organic farming methods for local farmers.
